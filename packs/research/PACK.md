# Research Pack

- **Status:** shipped
- **Consumer:** the operator's source-ingest and source-grounding workflow, plus a clean-room installer
- **Owner:** `w3rdist-creator`
- **Activation trigger:** explicit installation for a source-grounded research workflow
- **Kill condition:** uninstall or demote the pack when two consecutive reviews find no downstream decision, artifact, or question changed while review cost remains non-zero
- **Vault payload:** `vault/`, copied relative to a target vault root
- **Source disposition:** consolidates accepted public mechanisms; the Second Brain OS Powerpack is the primary mechanism source and does not ship as a separate pack

## Core contract

The pack ships a bounded source queue, immutable source snapshots, source notes with all five authority fields, a claim/evidence ledger, provenance and falsifiers, a first-class non-connection route, search-before-create and merge-before-duplicate behavior, and a validated two-domain pattern note. The dated seed chain is reviewed in `vault/Research/Reviews/Review - Bounded Selection Pressure.md`.
