# Source Note — Loop Scoreboard

## Source coordinate

CSV row 279 at `166e370850502c1878195ba122ab2ec82b2138d5`, captured 2026-07-10.

## Source fact

The source calls for recurring loops to be scored by decision impact, signal, burden, false-positive risk, time saved, artifact production, and attention cost, followed by a closed verdict.

## Interpretation allowed

It supports an operational-domain example in which visible review and a closed disposition apply selection pressure to recurring generation.

## Interpretation forbidden

It does not show that the same scoring scale transfers to source research or that every useful research process should recur.

## Next evidence required

Map causal links separately across the research and operations domains and record a falsifier plus non-applicability.

## Declared decision authority

This source may change the seed pattern's operational-domain map and review fields. It cannot establish a universal principle or authorize a live scheduler change.

## Caveat and strongest objection

Scorecards can proxy real usefulness and reward legible activity while missing quiet value.
