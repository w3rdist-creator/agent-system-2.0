# Citation Use and Demotion Rule

The citation/use log records a source or pattern only when it changes a named artifact, decision, experiment, or question. Citation count alone is not use, and repeated citations to one upstream source remain one evidentiary line.

At `review_due`, choose `keep | merge | demote | supersede | reject`. Demote a pattern from supported to candidate when it has no downstream use in two consecutive review windows, its decision delta disappears, its source becomes stale for the claim, or a falsifier is observed. Preserve the prior state and reason; never delete the correction trail.

Demotion removes promotion authority, not provenance. A later use may reopen the pattern only with current evidence and a new decision delta.
