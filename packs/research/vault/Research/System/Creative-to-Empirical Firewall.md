# Creative-to-Empirical Firewall

Fiction, metaphor, speculative history, design prompts, and personal observation may immediately support creative generation or question formation. They may not establish a factual target-domain claim.

To cross into empirical promotion, require independent target-domain source contact, one counterexample or failed transfer, an explicit causal mechanism, a falsifier, and non-applicability. Label every unsupported causal link `analogy-only` until then.

If the material remains creatively useful but fails empirical contact, keep it in the creative artifact and record `non_connection` for the factual bridge. Creative usefulness is not downgraded; evidentiary authority remains bounded.
