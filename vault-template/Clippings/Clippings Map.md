# Clippings Map

Clippings holds excerpts awaiting source-level treatment. A clipping must retain its coordinate and route to [[Sources/Sources Map]] or be rejected; it cannot become a claim by repetition. The seed chain skips a separate clipping because the complete public artifact is short enough to preserve directly, a real example of using the smallest sufficient surface.
