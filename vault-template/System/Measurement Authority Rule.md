# Measurement Authority Rule

A coordinate, dashboard, score, evaluation, calendar, or dataset may preserve evidence, route attention, constrain a claim, or trigger review. It may not decide beyond declared authority.

Every source-facing artifact distinguishes:

1. source fact;
2. interpretation allowed;
3. interpretation forbidden;
4. next evidence required;
5. state or decision the measurement is authorized to change.

Source grounding is not source obedience. The worked example is [[Sources/Source Note - Truth-Seeking Engine]].
