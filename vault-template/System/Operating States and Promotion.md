# Operating States and Promotion

Source state is one of `linked | captured | preserved | read | synthesized | applied | superseded | rejected`.

Knowledge state is one of `speculative | candidate | supported | challenged | canonical | superseded`.

Status records workflow position; it does not create truth. The promotion ladder is:

`raw capture → routed capture → candidate connection → active synthesis → project application → feedback/scoring → validated artifact → canonical operating knowledge`

At every step, material may be merged, demoted, archived, or recorded as a non-connection. Promotion requires provenance, strongest objection, falsifier or caveat, application value, and `decision_delta`: the concrete artifact, decision, rule, or future extraction behavior that changes.

Canonical operating knowledge additionally records scope, boundaries, failure modes, the strongest surviving objection, a blindspot or critique link, observed use, a demotion rule, and rollback or merge path. Canonical status is revoked when its demotion rule fires; history remains available through [[System/Belief Revision Rule]].
