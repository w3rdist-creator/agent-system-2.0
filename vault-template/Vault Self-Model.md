# Vault Self-Model

## Five layers

1. **Operating maps and system rules** — [[Home]] and [[System/System Rules]] state navigation, authority, and lifecycle contracts.
2. **Pack or domain landing pages** — optional installed packs own their bounded domain entry points; none are active in the base vault.
3. **Local section maps** — every top-level folder has one nearest map that explains membership and exit routes.
4. **Content and decision artifacts** — source notes, synthesis, projects, decisions, evidence, and reviews record work rather than merely categorize it.
5. **Immutable or append-only source snapshots** — [[Raw/Raw Map]] preserves provenance without dominating active navigation.

## Raw boundary

Raw snapshots are immutable or append-only after capture. Active synthesis may point to Raw only through a labeled provenance field, normally via a source note. Raw and Archive remain retrievable, but neither is allowed to become the active graph's routing center.

## Link discipline

Use wikilinks for navigation, hierarchy, provenance, or a real semantic relationship. Prefer the nearest local map. Use generated indexes for exhaustive inventories and avoid backlinks added only to inflate connectivity. A future target is explicit as `[[Future Note|planned]]`; an ordinary unresolved link is an error.

## Promotion and correction

The state vocabulary and promotion ladder live in [[System/Operating States and Promotion]]. Status never creates truth. Promotion needs provenance, strongest objection, falsifier or caveat, application value, and a `decision_delta`. Canonical knowledge also needs scope, boundaries, failure modes, observed use, demotion rules, and a rollback or merge path. Corrections append through [[System/Belief Revision Rule]] rather than silently replacing prior states.

## Similarity classification before merge

Classify high-similarity notes before editing:

- `duplicate` — same claim and role; merge with provenance.
- `expected hierarchy` — local detail legitimately restates a parent map.
- `bridge` — similar language connects distinct domains through a stated mechanism.
- `disagreement` — claims conflict and both evidence paths must remain visible.
- `reconciliation candidate` — conflict may dissolve after scope or terminology is aligned.
- `noise` — resemblance has no navigational, causal, or decision value.

Search before create, classify before merge, and preserve disagreement when evidence has not resolved it.
